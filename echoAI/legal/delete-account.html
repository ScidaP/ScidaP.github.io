Delete Your Echo AI Account

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Use this page to request permanent deletion of your Echo AI account and associated app data. You can also email support@echoai.app directly.

What will be deleted

  • Your Echo AI account profile.
  • Your voice entries and uploaded audio files.
  • Transcripts, summaries, analysis, scores, patterns, and insights associated with your account.
  • Account links associated with Echo AI, subject to provider and legal retention requirements.

What may be retained temporarily

Some records may remain in backups, logs, fraud-prevention records, security records, or legal compliance records for a limited period. These retained records are not used to provide the active app experience.

Request deletion

Enter the email connected to your Echo AI account. If you use a guest account, include any information that can help identify it, such as the display name used in the app and approximate date of last use.

Verification

We may need to verify that you control the account before deletion. Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days after verification.

Important

Account deletion is permanent. Once deletion is completed, your recordings, summaries, insights, and account data cannot be restored.
